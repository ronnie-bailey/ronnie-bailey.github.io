Professionally

Professionally, my career has been in enterprise identity security. Over time, that work became my profession; the study of language, culture, and public institutions remained a parallel pursuit.

Although they developed along different paths, each has informed the other. One became my vocation. The other continues as a lifelong course of study.

This archive documents that second path and the interests that continue to shape how I approach society, institutions, and international studies. My professional work is documented at ronniebailey.cloud.

My interest in language began independently in 2008 while I was still in high school. What started as personal study gradually expanded into formal education, public service, and a broader interest in the institutions, history, and cultures that shape international engagement.

This archive follows that progression — not as a résumé, but as a record of study, civic participation, and the experiences that continue to inform my professional perspective.
