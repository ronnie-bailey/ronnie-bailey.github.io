Global Mindset

My background brings together technical depth in identity security with a long-standing interest in language, diplomacy, human rights, public service, and cross-cultural communication.

The result is not a traditional international affairs profile. It is a global technology profile: practical security experience supported by cultural awareness, public-sector exposure, and international curiosity.
