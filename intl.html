This page documents a long-running interest in international affairs, language, public institutions, human rights, and cultural exchange. It is not presented as a claim of formal diplomatic service.

The record shows a consistent pattern: studying French, participating in global language-learning communities, engaging with public institutions, completing human-rights training, and exploring cultural and historical spaces across different settings.
