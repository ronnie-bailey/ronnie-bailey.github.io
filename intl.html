My interest in language and international affairs began independently in 2008 while I was still in high school. What started as personal study gradually expanded through language-learning communities, formal education, public service, travel, and continued independent research.

My professional interests center on the ways language, culture, history, and public institutions shape relationships between people and nations. This portfolio documents that progression as an ongoing record of study, service, and professional development.
