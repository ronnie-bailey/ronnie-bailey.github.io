My international interests developed through practical, documented experiences: French coursework at Alliance Française de Portland, language exchange work with Tokyo-based Lang-8, international relations study at Clark College, French Club leadership, human-rights training, and public service at the U.S. Capitol.

This page gathers that thread in one place. It is not a claim of diplomatic rank or foreign-service experience. It is a record of cultural formation, language study, public-facing service, and sustained engagement with international themes.
